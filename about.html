About This Project

This project is part of our Final Requirements for the Course Science Technology and Society, it aims to explore real-life ethical dilemmas by presenting sample cases, encouraging critical thinking, and allowing users to examine situations where there is no clear “right” or “wrong” answer.

Purpose of This Website

Ethical dilemmas appear everywhere — in technology, healthcare, environment, business, and daily interactions. Our goal is to provide scenarios that encourage:

  • Value analysis and responsible decision-making
  • Improved awareness of consequences
  • Balanced reasoning instead of emotional judgment
