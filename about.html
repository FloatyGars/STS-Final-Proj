About This Project

This project is part of our Final Requirements for the course Science, Technology, and Society. It is designed to explore real-life ethical dilemmas by presenting carefully selected sample cases through an interactive website. The platform encourages critical thinking by allowing users to engage with complex scenarios where there is often no clear “right” or “wrong” answer. By navigating these cases, users can examine different perspectives, reflect on the societal and technological implications of each situation, and better understand the nuanced decisions that shape our modern world.

Purpose of This Website

Ethical dilemmas emerge in nearly every aspect of life — from advances in technology and challenges in healthcare, to decisions affecting the environment, business, and even our everyday interactions. These situations often require individuals to navigate conflicting values, competing interests, and uncertain outcomes. Our goal is to present scenarios that stimulate thoughtful reflection and promote:

  • Value analysis and responsible decision-making
  • Improved awareness of consequences
  • Balanced reasoning instead of emotional judgment
